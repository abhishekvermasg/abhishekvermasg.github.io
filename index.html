Devi Nava-Yatri: A 9-Day Spiritual Retreat

Program Overview

Embark on a transformative journey inspired by the divine path of Parvati. This retreat combines Seva (selfless service), Bhakti (devotion), and Sadhana (spiritual practice), offering participants a chance to reconnect with their inner strength and wisdom.

Benefits

  • Discover your purpose and path.
  • Cultivate self-love and acceptance.
  • Strengthen your connection to the divine feminine.
  • Build a supportive community of like-minded women.

Itinerary

Day 1-3: Varanasi

Experience Seva through practical service activities, learning slokas, and Siddha Yoga practices.

Day 4-6: Kanchi

Deepen your devotion with temple visits, rituals, and cultural insights.

Day 7-9: Thiruvannamalai

Engage in spiritual practices such as Giri Pradakshina and meditation in a powerful, sacred environment.

Logistics

  • Included: Accommodation, meals, internal transport, temple visits, and teachings.
  • Not Included: Travel to Varanasi and return travel from Chennai/Thiruvannamalai.
